Browsing: You can browse events via either the timeline or the map. To browse using the timeline interface, left-click on the timeline and drag it to the right to go back in time, and drag it to the left to go forward in time. To browse using the map interface, pan the map by left-clicking and dragging the map, and zoom in and out of the map by rotating the mouse wheel.
Searching: Search below by typing search terms, and/or by limiting using the various filters that are available. Limiting options include date of event, event type, and country. To restart your search, click on "Reset All Filters", which will appear above the map after keywords and/or limiting filters have been selected.
